Budget Module - Test Case 7

Click Budget from the left navigation menu.
Wait until the URL becomes:https://forms.ecampusbuddy.com/index.php/m-budget
Click the All dropdown and select 1050.
Verify that the Budget vs. Actual chart (budgetDeptChart) updates after selecting 1050.
If the chart does not change, log the following alert in Report_Consolidated.md: "budgetDeptChart is not changed."
Verify that the DoughnutChart under Spend Distribution also updates.
If the chart does not change, log: "DoughnutChart is not changed."
Scroll down and verify that the Department Breakdown section is visible.
Under Department Breakdown, verify that the following columns are visible:
	Cost Center
	Budgeted
	Actual Spent
	Variance
	Utilization
If any of these are not visible, log: "Department Breakdown section is not visible."
Scroll back to the top of the page and click Detailed Listing.
Wait until the URL becomes: https://forms.ecampusbuddy.com/index.php/m-budget/listing
Click the FY-2025 (Default) dropdown and verify that the year options are displayed.
If the options are not visible, log: "Year options are not visible."
Click Import Budget Data.
Verify that the following sections are visible:
	Financial Year
	Select CSV / Excel File
If either section is missing, log: "Import Budget Data sections are not visible."
Click Sample and verify that the sample CSV file is downloaded successfully.
If the download fails, log: "Sample CSV file is not downloading."
Click the FY-2025 dropdown and verify that the available year options are displayed.
If the options are not visible, log: "FY-2025 year options are not visible."
Click Choose File and upload the downloaded sample CSV file.
Click Upload.
Wait until the success message appears: "CSV uploaded successfully. (3 duplicate rows skipped)."
Measure the total upload time and record it in Report_Consolidated.md.
Expected upload time: 2 seconds
If the upload takes more than 2 seconds, log: "Uploading time is taking more than 2 seconds."
Under Budget Allocation Records, click Export CSV and verify that the CSV file is downloaded successfully.
If the export fails, log: "CSV file is not exporting."
Under Budget Allocation Records, verify that the following columns are visible:
	ID
	Cost Center
	Original Total
	-20%
	-15%
	-10%
	Created
	Actions
If any of these columns are missing, log the following alert in Report_Consolidated.md: "Required columns are not visible under Budget Allocation Records."

Important: Do not stop or fail the automation if any validation fails. Log the appropriate alert message in Report_Consolidated.md and continue executing the remaining test steps.
